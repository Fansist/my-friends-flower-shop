Our Story

A downtown studio built on personal touch

My Friend's Flower Shop is a downtown San Diego floral studio known for custom-designed arrangements spanning traditional, contemporary, and dramatic “over-the-top” styles. Owner the owner and his team hand-select every stem from trusted vendors for freshness and quality.

Regulars know the shop for going above and beyond, including meeting clients on days the shop is normally closed, and for thoughtful, personalized “Passion Florals” gifting and special-occasion pieces in the heart of the city.
